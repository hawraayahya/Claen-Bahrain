Clean & Green Bahrain

Clean & Grean Bahrain Initiative

Clean & Green Bahrain Initiative is a community-based environmental program that welcomes volunteers of all ages. Volunteers help with beach clean-ups, recycling activities, and tree planting to support a healthier and cleaner environment.

Questions Answers
Who can volunteer? Volunteer of all ages are welcome. Participants under 16 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.
What activities are included? Beach clean-ups, recycling campaigns, and tree planting activities.
How do I register? Fill out the volunteer form below or contact us via email.
